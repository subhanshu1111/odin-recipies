Pizza Bianaca

Description

Grilling pizza allows for a beautiful crisp crust, and this pizza bianca with mozzarella and prosciutto will also be finished before most pizzerias could deliver.

Ingridients

  • Pizza Dough
  • 1 table spoon of Olive Oil
  • 1 pinch Salt

Steps

  1. Preheat a gas grill with all burners on High for 10 to 15 minutes.
  2. Shape pizza dough into an 8 to 10-inch disc. Brush dough with olive oil.
