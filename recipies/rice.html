Rice

Description

These twist cheese sticks are easy and cheesy. This super simple recipe uses frozen puff pastry so there's no dough to make. They're very cool for entertaining.

Ingridients

  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed just enough to unfold
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil (preferably drained from a tin of anchovies)
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
  2. Unfold puff pastry into a flat sheet and brush olive oil over top. Season with salt, pepper, and cayenne pepper.
