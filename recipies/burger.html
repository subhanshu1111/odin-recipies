Description

Chicken Murphy is a hearty and flavor-packed stew made with Italian sausage, chicken thighs, potatoes, mushrooms, and sweet and spicy Peppadew peppers in a delicious garlic and white wine sauce. Itâ€™s comfort food with a kick!

Ingridients

  • 1 pound bulk Italian sausage
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  1. Cook sausage in a 5-to 6-quart Dutch oven over medium heat until browned, 6 to 8 minutes; transfer to a bowl with a slotted spoon.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in Dutch oven; add chicken and cook until lightly browned, 4 to 5 minutes. Transfer chicken to the bowl with sausage.
